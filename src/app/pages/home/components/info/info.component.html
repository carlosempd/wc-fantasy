Welcome to world cup fantasy footbal!

This is a simple web application that allows you to create your own team and play against other teams. You can create your own football team. For that, you will see a list of players from the different national teams, assign them to your team, and to save the team.

Rules

The game has a few rules you have to take into account when creating your team, please check the list below:

  • Maximum of 4 players from the same national team.
  • Team size limit to 16 players.
  • Each team must have one coach, and at least:
    • 4 defenders.
    • 4 midfielders.
    • 2 attackers.
    • 2 goalkeepers.
